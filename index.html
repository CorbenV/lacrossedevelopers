About

This thing is just starting to get going. The plan is to get a meetup group going for local developers, students, etc. to get together semi-regularly to chat, eventually hold mini tech talks, etc. We developers are relatively few in this part of the state, so let's band together.

There's some more detailed info about the idea here.

Events

First meetup was held November 7th, 2019. Only three of us this time around, but we had a good discussion and started planning the future of the group.

Second meetup is planned for Thursday, February 6, 2020 at 6:30pm at the south side's Goodwill in the community room. Join us in Discord for more information.
