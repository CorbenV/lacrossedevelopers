La Crosse, WI software developer meetup group

Meeting info

As of February 2022, meetings are held on the last Thursday evening of every month.

Meeting location will be posted in Discord.

We also post meeting info on the calendar at https://that.us/communities/la-crosse-area-software-developers/

About

This is a fairly new group that started in the fall of 2019. We are a laid-back group of programmers, entrepreneuers, tech enthusiasts, and game developers who hang out, discuss technology. We welcome everyone, from experienced programmers, to students, to people who aren't into programming but enjoy technology.

Discord
